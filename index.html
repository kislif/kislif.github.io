Growth Mindset

Individuals who believe their talents can be developed through hard work, good strategies, and input from others, have a growth mindset.
  • Sharpen the saw. Practise existing skills, learn new ones.
  • Seek first to understand, then to be understood.
  • Accept valid criticism as a gift.
  • Be grateful.
  • Set goals.
  • Be realistic about timelines.
  • Value the journey towards the destination.
  • Appreciate your strengths, and work to improve your weaknesses.
  • Acknowledge and embrace imperfection in yourself and others.
  • Respect for self and others.
  • Be willing to make mistakes in front of others.
  • Face your challenges bravely. Shift your perspective to see challenges as an opportunity or adventure.
  • Pay attention to your words and thoughts. Replace negative thoughts with more positive ones.
  • Don't seek approval from others. Cultivate self-acceptance and self-approval.
  • Be authentic.
  • Cultivate a sense of purpose.
